About

A lot can happen when we bring together government officials, city managers, technologists, area experts, and open data evangelists.

Conversations get started. Best practices are gleaned. Challenges are discussed.

The Open Data Roadshow began at the Inland Empire Data Forum. A small group from Code for America embarked on an open data journey (#opendatavan) from San Francisco to Riverside, CA to co-host an exciting day of open data panels, presentations, and workshops. The successful event has inspired the Open Data Roadshow.

Goals

  • Bringing the best of open data practices, policies and experiences to cities.
  • Educating a government audience to the concept, values, and opportunities of open data.
  • Consulting and troubleshooting how government staff can pursue open data within their own agencies.
  • Sharing resources and best practices to support governments interested in implementing open data.
